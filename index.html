Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity for HDPE pipes?

MOQ starts at 100 metres for standard sizes (20mm–110mm). For larger diameters (125mm+), MOQ is 50 metres. Bulk pricing available for project quantities.

What pressure rating should I choose for my project?

PN rating indicates max working pressure in bar at 20°C. PN 10 or PN 12.5 for residential supply. PN 16 for industrial or high-rise applications. Our technical team can advise based on your system requirements.

Are your HDPE pipes suitable for drinking water?

Yes. Our PE100 pipes are manufactured without heavy metal stabilisers and meet food-grade safety standards, certified for potable water under IS 4984.

What jointing methods are available?

Butt Fusion (63mm+), Electrofusion socket fittings (all sizes), and Mechanical compression fittings (small diameter coils). All create leak-proof permanent joints.

Do you provide installation support?

Yes — on-site guidance for large projects, fusion welding training, and a network of certified contractors across India for turnkey support.

Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

State-of-the-art extrusion technology ensuring consistent quality and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Virgin-grade PE100 and PE80 compounds from certified suppliers only. Each batch is tested for melt flow index, density, and carbon black content before production.

  • Virgin PE100 grade compound only
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion

Computer-controlled temperature zones ensure homogeneous melt. Wall thickness tolerance maintained within ±0.1mm across the full pipe length.

  • Temperature-controlled melt zones
  • Uniform wall thickness guaranteed

Controlled Cooling

Multi-stage water cooling baths gradually reduce pipe temperature, relieving internal stresses and preventing warping for dimensional stability.

  • Staged cooling prevents residual stress
  • Temperature-monitored water baths

Vacuum Sizing

Vacuum sizing tanks maintain the outer diameter while the pipe cools, ensuring exact dimensional compliance with ISO and IS standards every metre.

  • OD tolerance within ±0.1mm
  • Continuous laser diameter measurement

Quality Control Testing

Every pipe is hydrostatic tested at 1.5x rated pressure. Random batch samples go to our in-house lab for full mechanical property testing.

  • 100% hydrostatic pressure test
  • Full dimensional verification per batch

Laser Marking

Permanently laser-marked with product code, dimensions, pressure rating, batch number, and manufacture date for full traceability.

  • Full batch traceability on every pipe
  • ISO-compliant marking format

Precision Cutting

Automated planetary cutters produce square, burr-free ends critical for achieving proper fusion welds. Cut length accuracy ±5mm on standard lengths.

  • Square ends for reliable fusion joints
  • Deburring included as standard

Packaging & Dispatch

UV-wrapped and bundled for transport. Each shipment includes a quality certificate, hydrostatic test report, and dimensional inspection report.

  • UV-protective wrapping for storage
  • Full quality documentation included
